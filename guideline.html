arXiv Submission Guide for NeLaMKRR 2024

Follow these steps to properly submit your paper to arXiv for inclusion in the NeLaMKRR 2024 proceedings.

NeLaMKRR 2024 Workshop Information

The First International Workshop on Next-Generation Language Models for Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (NeLaMKRR 2024) is associated with the 21st International Conference on Principles of Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KR 2024). It aims to bring together researchers and practitioners to discuss novel techniques and innovative ideas at the intersection of language models, knowledge representation, and reasoning. The workshop will take place on November 4, 2024.

Proceedings of the workshop will be hosted on arXiv to support open research and give authors full control over the licensing of their work.

Submission Guide

  1. Check arXiv Listing:

    Visit this URL to locate your specific report number. You can double-check the result with the tool below:

  2. Choose a License:

    During the submission process, select a license for your work. You have full discretion over this choice, which could be a Creative Commons license or arXiv's standard license. NeLaMKRR does not influence this decision.

  3. Metadata Input:
    • Comments Field:

      In the Comments field, please include the following text:

      Comments: Presented at NeLaMKRR@KR, 2024 (arXiv:2410.05339)
    • Report Number Field:

      In the Report-no field, enter your specific report number, e.g., NeLaMKRR/2024/02.

    Example complete entry for referencing:

    		Comments: Presented at NeLaMKRR@KR, 2024 (arXiv:2410.05339)
		Report-no: YOUR_REPORT_NUMBER (e.g., NeLaMKRR/2024/02)

  An example of a submitted paper can be seen on arXiv here.

Important Note

You can submit your paper to arXiv at any time. However, please be aware that a post-review process will be conducted. If your paper is not presented at the workshop, or if there are other significant issues, it may be removed from the proceedings. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
